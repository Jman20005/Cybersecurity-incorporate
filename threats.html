THREATS

Understanding the Modern Digital Battlefield

Phishing

Fake emails or websites designed to trick users into revealing passwords, banking details, or personal data.

Ransomware

Malware that encrypts files and demands payment for their release. It can shut down hospitals, businesses, and governments.

Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS)

Overloads a server with traffic until it crashes, making websites and services unavailable.

Data Breaches

Unauthorized access to confidential data such as passwords, financial records, and medical information.
